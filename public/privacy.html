Privacy Policy

GeoQuest (“we”) is a geography game. This page describes what data we use and how.

Data we use

  • Leaderboard: If you enter a player name and submit a score, we store that name and score (and, if you sign in with Google, a link to your account) to show the leaderboard.
  • Google Sign-In: If you use “Sign in with Google,” we use Google’s service to identify you and link your scores to your account. We do not receive your password.
  • Ads: We use Google AdSense. Google may set cookies and use data to show ads and measure performance. See Google’s Privacy Policy and How Google uses data.

Your choices

You can play without entering a name (scores won’t be submitted). You can revoke ad personalization or consent via the consent choices shown on the site or in your browser/Google settings.

Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, use the contact or feedback option in the app or reach out via the repository/site owner.
